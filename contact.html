    Tipical work stage

  • Our typical work stages are designed to give our clients the necessary basics to get building.
  • You email us details including your name, project address, council name, the below service package required, project scope, and approved planning drawings where available.
  • We use ordinance survey and 3D bird's-eye view mapping of your property to assess your project against planning policy and/or any relevant planning history in your street to check if there is a reasonable chance of gaining permission.
  • We visit your property to undertake a measured survey and discuss design options.
  • We produce the measured survey drawings in approximately one week.
  • You provide us with a design brief.
  • We produce the first draft planning and/or building regulations drawings (excluding structural calculations) depending on which package you choose, in approximately one week.
  • We revise the drawings if required by you.
  • We submit the planning application(s) and send you council fee payment details.
  • We act as you application agent and revise the drawings if required by the council.
  • We refer you to consultants such as structural engineers for structural calculations, approved inspectors for building regulations approval, party wall surveyors for party wall agreements, or project managers to manage the construction if required.

    • We revise the drawings if required by your consultants.
